Krucheniki

Meat rolls from pork or beef tenderloin. The meat is cut into thin pieces. beat and wrap in it a filling of mushrooms, boiled eggs with greens, and rice. Then they are fried and stewed until done on a low heat. For those who are "in favor" of non-standard flavors, you should try Volyn krucheniki made of sauerkraut and with chicken and prunes.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

50 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Hot dishes All Food

instructions

step 1

Let's fight. To begin, cut the steak with a thickness of no more than 1.5 cm. Beat the meat on both sides and season with salt and pepper.

step 2

For the filling, you need to cut onions and mushrooms into small pieces. First, fry the onion in a pan in butter, then add the mushrooms and fry until the liquid evaporates completely. Generously sprinkle with chopped garlic and herbs. Cool down.

step 3

Mix grated cheese with mushrooms. Salt and pepper. Put the filling on each piece of meatloaf. Wrap with a roll and fasten with a toothpick or thread so that the kruchenyk keeps its shape during frying. Fry until golden brown.

step 4

After frying, krucheniki with mushrooms should be stewed in broth for 30 minutes. 10 minutes before readiness, mix flour and sour cream and add to the broth with krucheniki. Simmer for 10 minutes. Serve with side dish and sour cream sauce.

ingredients

Meat for chops 600-700 g.

Chopped champignons 500 g.

Onion 1 pc.

Hard cheese 60 g.

Garlic 3-4 cloves.

Sour cream 21% 150 g.

Flour 1 tsp.

Butter 73% 20 g.

Vegetable oil 2-3 tbsp. l.

Salt, pepper to taste

Greens 1 bunch

tools

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Cups

Deep Bowl
