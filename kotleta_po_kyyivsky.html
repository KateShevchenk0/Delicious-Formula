Kotleta po-kyyivsʹky

A golden crispy crust, tender chicken meat and a juicy filling inside - you will fall in love with the Kiev-style cutlet from the first time. Basically, it is a chicken cutlet, rolled into a roll together with greens and butter, fried in a deep fryer. It is served in a special way, with a chicken bone, which relies exclusively on a decorative function. The side dish can be any: boiled potatoes, vegetable puree or rice.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Hot dishes All Food

instructions

step 1

Cut the skinless chicken into two pieces of breast fillet together with the shoulder bone on the leg. Place the fillet with the inner side up, make a cut from the middle lengthwise on both sides, place the halves so that the filling fits, hit with a hammer. Carefully remove the white tendons.

step 2

In a bowl, mix chopped parsley, melted butter and salt until smooth. Divide the mixture into two parts and put in the freezer for 5 minutes. Then put the filling in the fillet, wrap it tightly and put it back in the freezer for 5 minutes.

step 3

Separately, beat eggs with milk in a bowl. Roll cutlets in flour, then in egg mixture, in breadcrumbs, then in mixture, then again in breadcrumbs. Pour oil into a frying pan and heat to 200 degrees.

step 4

Heat the oven to 200 degrees. Deep-fry the cutlets until golden brown (about 5 minutes), then cook in the oven for 10 minutes. Before serving, put on a napkin to absorb the fat.

ingredients

Butter - 140 g.

Chicken - 1.2 kg.

Parsley - 20 g.

Salt - 0.5 teaspoon.

Chicken egg - 2 pcs.

Milk - 100 ml.

Wheat flour - 60 g.

Breadcrumbs - 140 g.

Butter - 50 g.

Vegetable oil - 300 ml.

tools

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Cups

Deep Bowl

Frying pan
