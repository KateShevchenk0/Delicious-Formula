Nalysniki

Thin pancakes with a beautiful openwork mesh are a symbol of the national holiday of Shrovetide. They are prepared in milk with the addition of wheat or buckwheat flour, eggs and sugar. There are also options for a sourdough test, then they turn out lush. You can eat them simply with sour cream, jam, honey, or wrap a filling in them. Here the choice is just as diverse: salty and sweet cheese; fried mushrooms with onions and hard cheese; spinach with cream cheese; red fish with ricotta, mascarpone. Chicken liver and boiled eggs, sour milk cheese and berries, poppy seeds and dried fruits are also combined in the fillings. Filled pancakes must be ground in the oven or deep pan before serving.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Hot dishes Dessert All Food

instructions

step 1

WAdd salt and sugar to sifted flour, mix thoroughly. Pour warm milk into another container, add one egg and vegetable oil. Mix the two containers together and leave the dough to rest for 2 hours.

step 2

When the specified time is up, add a little water to the dough. In the meantime, prepare the filling: take cheese, sugar and eggs, beat them into a creamy mass (if desired, add vanilla).

step 3

Pour the washed corn grits into the boiling liquid. Salt and pepper the porridge to taste.

step 4

Fry the flatbreads on medium heat in a pan greased with oil.

step 5

Put a tablespoon of filling on each flatbread. Put it in the center of the pancake and spread it over the entire surface. Turn the pancake into a tube.

step 6

Put all the obtained flatbreads in a container. Place pieces of butter between them. Generously sprinkle with sugar. Heat the oven to 50Â°C and send the pancakes to it for 2-3 hours. And then you can enjoy delicious flatbreads with sour cream, jam, condensed milk or some other favorite topping.

ingredients

Eggs - 4 pc.

Sugar - 1 tbsp.

Milk - 2 tbsp.

Flour - 2 tbsp.

Salt - 1 tsp.

Water - 1 tbsp.

Cheese - 500 g.

Butter - 200 g

tools

Frying pan

Measuring Cups

Deep Bowl
