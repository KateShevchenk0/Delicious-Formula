Vareniks

In most cases, varenyki are prepared from fresh dough in milk, but in some regions it is replaced with kefir. Such dumplings turn out more lush and tender. Choose one or more components for the filling. A combination with cheese, cherry, raspberry is popular. They are also made with meat, mushrooms, vegetables and nettles. Butter is usually used for filling sweet dumplings, and frying with onions and crackers is used for others. You can additionally pour sour cream.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Hot dishes Dessert All Food

instructions

step 1

Beat an egg into slightly heated water, add salt and mix thoroughly. Gradually add flour and mix well so that the result is a fairly dense and elastic dough. We wrap it in a film and leave it for 30-40 minutes. "reach".

step 2

At this time, we prepare the filling, for which we boil peeled potatoes in slightly salted water. Clean and chop the onion, which should be lightly saut√©ed in vegetable oil until golden. Mash the hot potatoes well and combine with fried onions, pepper to taste.

step 3

Knead the dough once more and prepare portioned "cakes" in a way that is convenient for you, into which we place the slightly cooled filling. Carefully fasten the edges of the dough, and boil the dumplings with potatoes for 5-7 minutes. (until they float) in slightly salted water. The finished dish with sour cream tastes especially good.

ingredients

Cheese - 400 gr.

Eggs - 2 pcs.

Sugar - 2 tbsp.

Semolina - 4-5 tbsp.

Vanilla sugar - 1 tsp.

Salt - 0.5 tsp.

Flour - 1-1.5 cups.

tools

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Cups

Deep Bowl
