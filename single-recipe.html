Banosh

Describing Ukrainian national dishes, it is impossible not to add to the list the Hutsul dish - banosh. The basis of this dish is corn grits, which is cooked in milk and cream for several hours in the oven. The brightness of the taste is added by fried cracklings, bacon and salty cheese, cheese. Mushroom sauce is also added if desired. The combination of ingredients is unusual, but tasty. According to ancient traditions, banush should be cooked only by men and on a bonfire, as shepherds used to do.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

120 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Hot dishes Mush All Food

instructions

step 1

Wash the groats 2-3 times under running water.

step 2

Pour water into a saucepan and bring to a boil. Add milk and sour cream to the water, bring to a boil over medium heat.

step 3

Pour the washed corn grits into the boiling liquid. Salt and pepper the porridge to taste.

step 4

Cook the banoche until ready, until the porridge starts to easily come away from the sides of the pan (about 25-30 minutes).

step 5

After that, add other ingredients to the porridge to taste - cheese, lard, mushrooms, etc.

ingredients

Corn grits - 100 g.

Fat sour cream - 250 ml.

Milk - 200 ml.

Water - 100 ml.

Salt and pepper to taste.

tools

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Cups

Deep Bowl
