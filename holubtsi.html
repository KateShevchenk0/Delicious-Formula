Holubtsi

Real Ukrainian cabbage rolls are made from slightly boiled or pickled cabbage leaves. Before the appearance of rice, the basis for the filling was made from corn porridge and millet, to which meat, lard and fried onions were added. Today, the classic filling uses minced pork and beef, which is mixed with rice or corn grits. Garlic, parsley, bell pepper, porcini mushrooms, spices are added as desired. Depending on the regions, the sizes of cabbage rolls also differ: the Hutsul ones are large, the size of a palm, and the South-Eastern ones are miniature, the size of a little finger.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

120 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Hot dishes All Food

instructions

step 1

Separate the cabbage into leaves, place in a bowl and add salt. Boil the kettle, pour boiling water over the leaves, cover and leave for 15-20 minutes.

step 2

Wash the rice well and boil it in plenty of salted water until half cooked, 10-12 minutes. Then throw it in a colander and rinse with cold water.

step 3

Peel and grate the carrot on a large grater. Peel the onion, cut into cubes.

step 4

Heat the pan, pour vegetable oil. Fry onions and carrots over medium heat, stirring, for 1-2 minutes. Add rice to the minced meat, salt and pepper. Then add egg, frying and finely chopped greens. Mix well

step 5

Cut large cabbage leaves in half. Put a small amount of minced meat on the prepared cabbage leaves and wrap it in the form of an envelope. Put in a saucepan.

step 6

Preparation of sauce. Boil a kettle, dilute the sour cream with water and tomato paste (150 g of sour cream per 0.5 l of water), add salt.

step 7

Pour sauce over cabbage rolls. Stew them at a gentle boil under the lid until the minced meat is ready, about 50-60 minutes.

ingredients

White cabbage - 1 pc.

Minced meat - 600 g.

Rice - 0.5 tbsp.

Egg - 1 pc.

Onions - 1-2 pcs.

Carrot - 1-2 pcs.

Sour cream - 150-200 g.

Tomato paste - 1 tsp.

Oil - 30 g.

Ground black pepper - a pinch.

Salt - to taste.

tools

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Cups

Deep Bowl
